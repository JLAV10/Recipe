Classic Fried Chicken

This classic fried chicken recipe is crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside. It’s easy to make and perfect for a comforting homemade meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 pieces of chicken
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 2 eggs
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Mix flour, salt, pepper, and paprika in a bowl.
  3. Beat eggs in a separate bowl.
  4. Dip chicken in eggs, then coat with flour mixture.
  5. Fry chicken until golden brown and fully cooked.
  6. Remove and drain on paper towels before serving.
